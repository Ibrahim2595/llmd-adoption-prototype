description: "Configuring llm-d for RL training pipelines and reward model serving."

Reinforcement learning workloads use LLMs as policy or reward models inside training loops, generating large batches of rollouts at high throughput with relaxed latency constraints. llm-d's wide expert parallelism and asynchronous processing features are particularly well-suited to this workload pattern.
