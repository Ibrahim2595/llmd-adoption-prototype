Chatbot deployments require consistent low latency, high concurrency, and graceful handling of bursty traffic — while often sharing conversation context across turns. llm-d's intelligent inference scheduling, prefix-cache-aware routing, and flow control features are designed to meet the stringent SLOs of interactive chat applications at scale.
