---
title: "Agentic Inference"
description: "Optimizing llm-d for agentic workloads with multi-turn, tool-calling patterns."
sidebar_order: 2
---

Agentic workloads involve multi-turn conversations where the model repeatedly calls tools, processes intermediate results, and generates follow-up requests. These workloads have distinctive traffic patterns — bursty, correlated, and often with long shared context prefixes — that llm-d's prefix-cache-aware scheduling and disaggregated serving are well-positioned to optimize.
