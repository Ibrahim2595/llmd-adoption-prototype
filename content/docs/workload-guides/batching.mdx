---
title: "Batching & Job-Scoped Routing"
description: "Using llm-d's batching capabilities for throughput-optimized workloads."
sidebar_order: 4
---

Batch inference workloads process large numbers of requests offline or asynchronously, prioritizing throughput over per-request latency. llm-d's asynchronous processing mode and job-scoped routing allow you to fill idle accelerator capacity efficiently while isolating batch traffic from latency-sensitive online serving.
