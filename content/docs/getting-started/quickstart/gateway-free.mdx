---
title: "Gateway-Free Quickstart"
description: "Deploy llm-d without an inference gateway in minutes."
sidebar_order: 1
---

The gateway-free quickstart is the fastest way to get llm-d running on a Kubernetes cluster. It deploys vLLM replicas with the llm-d scheduling sidecar but without a full inference gateway, making it ideal for evaluation and development environments with limited resources.
